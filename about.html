Our Mission

At Save Ocean Life, we are committed to raising awareness about the critical state of our oceans. We strive to educate the public, promote sustainable practices, and support conservation efforts to ensure a vibrant and healthy marine ecosystem.

What We Do

We encourages responsible fishery management and sustainable fishing practices to protect the future of fishing communities, preserve ocean ecosystems, and ensure seafood is available to everyone, always.

  1. Fisheres can volunteer to be assesd by our fish standard.
  2. If they meet our requirement, they are permitted to sell their products with our special label.
  3. Restaurants can choose to source our certified sustainable seafood, and we will make sure consumers will get our certified products.
  4. Consumers will buy our products and increase our demand for sustainable products.
  5. This can finally lead more fisheries to choose our brand and thus create a more sustainable seafood market.
